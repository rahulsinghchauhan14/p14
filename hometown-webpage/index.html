Gurgaon, Haryana

Location

Situated in the northern Indian state of Haryana, Gurgaon is a thriving city that forms a crucial part of the National Capital Region (NCR). Located approximately 30 kilometers southwest of New Delhi, Gurgaon has emerged as a prominent business and technology hub.

About

Infrastructure and Modernity

Gurgaon, often referred to as the "Millennium City," is a testament to modern urban development. Boasting a skyline adorned with skyscrapers, the city is home to some of India's leading multinational corporations and IT companies.

Cultural Fusion

Despite its contemporary vibe, Gurgaon retains a rich cultural essence. The city's cosmopolitan population contributes to a diverse cultural tapestry, reflected in its festivals, art exhibitions, and culinary offerings.

Economic Dynamism

Renowned for its flourishing business landscape, Gurgaon hosts a plethora of industries, including finance, technology, and manufacturing. The city's economic dynamism has attracted professionals and businesses from around the world.

Things To Do

  • Explore Sultanpur National Park for birdwatching
  • Visit the Heritage Transport Museum to delve into India's transportation history
  • Unwind at Damdama Lake for boating and adventure
  • Experience live entertainment at Kingdom of Dreams
  • Play golf at renowned courses like DLF Golf and Country Club
